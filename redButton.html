About our Supported charities:

Independent Age

Founded over 150 years ago, Independent Age offers older people regular friendly contact, a strong campaigning voice and free and impartial advice on the issues that matter: care and support, money and benefits, health and mobility.

independentage.org

Make-A-Wish® UK

Make-A-Wish grants magical wishes to enrich the lives of seriously ill children and young people. These wishes provide hope for the future, strength to fight their condition and the chance to create memories to treasure forever. (Charity Reg Nos. 295672/SC037479)

make-a-wish.org.uk

Guide Dogs

Every hour someone in the UK loses their sight and a guide dog can really transform the quality of life for a blind or partially sighted person. It's Guide Dogs mission to help people who are blind or partially sighted enjoy the same freedom of movement as others do.

guidedogs.org.uk

Mind

Mind is the leading mental health charity in England and Wales. They won’t give up until everyone with a mental health problem has the support they deserve. The charity provides advice and services to those suffering with mental health problems, they campaign to improve services and raise awareness.

mind.org.uk
